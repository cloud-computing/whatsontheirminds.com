What's On Their Minds?

This project was created as part of the Rewired State hack day,
by Erin Staniland, Tom ten Thij and David Salgado.

Choose an MP from the list to see a tag cloud of the terms that occur in their most recent parliamentary written questions.

The data is pulled from They Work For You and the Yahoo! term extraction web service is used to find interesting terms to count.

Cool features may follow.
